SalesPilot—Your AI Multi-Agent Sales Assistant

SalesPilot lets sales teams ask questions about their customers and get answers pulled directly from their database and contracts, with every fact sourced. Because right now, one simple question means four tools, two people, and 30 minutes — SalesPilot collapses all of that into one text box.

A portfolio project by Nan Xu — multi-agent orchestration with LangGraph, source-grounded RAG, and the Claude API.  ·  GitHub: github.com/nxu22  ·  nanxu.site
